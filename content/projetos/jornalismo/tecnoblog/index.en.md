{{< lead >}}
[Tecnoblog](https://tecnoblog.net) is one of Brazil's largest and most respected technology journalism outlets, with in-depth coverage of gadgets, apps, business, and digital culture. A reference for millions of readers, the outlet stands out for its editorial quality and specialized technology journalism.
{{< /lead >}}

From 2020 to 2023, Bruno worked as a reporter at Tecnoblog, developing specialized coverage of technology and business. During this period, he produced investigative reports, in-depth analyses, and SEO-optimized news stories, building relationships with industry sources and covering in-person events.

He was part of the **Tecnocast** cast, Tecnoblog's official podcast, participating in episodes on the major topics in the technology industry. He also worked on the production and presentation of video content.

In 2022, he traveled to **Buenos Aires, Argentina**, to cover the launch of the Motorola Edge 30 Ultra on-site — one of the most prominent international coverage pieces of the period. Throughout his time at the outlet, he tracked content metrics and performance to guide editorial strategy.
