{{< lead >}}
[Canaltech](https://canaltech.com.br) is one of Brazil's leading technology journalism portals, focused on news, analysis, and tutorials about technology, gaming, science, and innovation. Part of the Magalu ecosystem, the outlet reaches millions of readers monthly and is a reference in the country's tech media segment.
{{< /lead >}}

In January 2024, Bruno joined the portal as a reporter. In recognition of his exceptional performance and contributions throughout the year, he was awarded the Canaltech Outstanding Professional award, celebrating exclusive reports and interviews, highly relevant journalistic coverage, and integration into strategic projects that put Canaltech in the spotlight in Brazilian media.

The recognition culminated in an invitation to become editor in October of the same year, consolidating his position as one of the most important voices in technology journalism in Brazil and reaffirming his commitment to editorial excellence and innovation in journalism.

In 2025, he further expanded his international presence by attending the realme GT 7 launch in Paris, the Expand North Star and GITEX Global events in Dubai — one of the world's largest technology and innovation gatherings — and AWS re:Invent in Las Vegas, United States, Amazon Web Services' flagship cloud computing conference.

Throughout Brazil, he holds credentials from Web Summit Rio, Rio Innovation Week, Rock in Rio, and other conferences.

He also produced and hosted exclusive interviews on the Canaltech Podcast, a space dedicated to discussing trends, launches, and the impact of technological innovations.

In addition, he appeared on CNN Brasil segments, sharing market analysis on technology, and on Porta 101, a program that covered the behind-the-scenes of the tech industry at Canaltech.
